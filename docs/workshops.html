Workshops

Since 2017, I have been organizing the annual Stockholm Uppsala Education Economics Workshop. I am also involved in the organization of the IFAU-UCLS Methods Workshop series at IFAU. Previously, I organized the weekly research seminar and the brownbag seminar series at SOFI at Stockholm University and the weekly research seminars IFAU.

I have also been acting as a guest reviewer for the Colloquium on Personnel Economics 2025 in Trondheim and 2026 in Rotterdam

Stockholm Uppsala Education Economics Workshop

The Stockholm Uppsala Education Economics Workshop series is held at rotating institutions in Stockholm and Uppsala. The aim of the workshop is bringing together researchers with an interest in the economics of education and creating a regional network.

The workshop receives funding from the Jan Wallanders och Tom Hedelius stiftelse samt Tore Browaldhs stiftelse (grant numbers Fv19-0002 and Fh24-0017).
